## Get in Touch

We'd love to hear from you — whether you have a story tip, a question about the township, or just want to say hello.

## About This Site

London Grove Commons is an independent community project. We are not affiliated with the township government.

If you'd like to reach the people behind this site, have a story idea, or want to get involved, drop us a line — we're always happy to hear from neighbors.
