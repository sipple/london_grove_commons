## What Is London Grove Commons?

London Grove Commons is a community project of London Grove Township that opens a window on the sometimes small, but always impactful, ways your local government makes life in London Grove better for everyone. Every day, committees, officials, and community groups are at work in our community. It's easy to miss how much their efforts matter -- and that's why we're bringing their stories to you here.

For official township information, like upcoming events, meeting agendas, and tax information, please visit [londongrove.org](https://www.londongrove.org).

## What We Do

We follow township committees into their work. We walk the land with planners, and talk with the people who volunteer their time to serve the community. Then we share what we learn through the stories on this site.

## Why Stories?

Meeting minutes are important. But they don't capture the moments those meetings make real; when our community gardens are replanted for the spring, or when our public works team gives their weekend to the town to keep our roads clear. Those human moments are what make local government meaningful — and they're what we aim to share.
