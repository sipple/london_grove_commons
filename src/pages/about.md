## What Is London Grove Commons?

London Grove Commons is an independent community project that covers local government in London Grove Township, Pennsylvania. We're not affiliated with the township — we're residents who believe local government deserves the same quality of attention that bigger stories get.

## What We Do

We follow township committees into their work. We sit in on meetings, walk the land with planners, and talk with the people who volunteer their time to serve the community. Then we share what we learn, in plain language, through the stories on this site.

## Why Stories?

Meeting minutes are important. But they don't capture the moment when a planning commissioner kneels down to check the soil, or the way a parks volunteer lights up when talking about a new trail. Those human moments are what make local government meaningful — and they're what we aim to share.

## This Is Not an Official Township Website

London Grove Commons is an independent project. For official township information, please visit [londongrove.org](https://www.londongrove.org).
